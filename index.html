Villanueva Coffee Shop

From the past ages, cofee has never let humans down. Humans let cofee down. Humans made sugar and creamer, that affects the true taste of coffee. The strong aromatic smell and the bitterness of each sip are replaced by sweet, disgusting taste of manufactured products.

All that is left by the passing of generation is brewed coffee. Brewed coffee has never been replaced in the heart of many. Especially brewed coffee without preservatives. Humans are funny creatures. They degrade quality of coffee with the passing years.

However, here in Villanueva's Coffee Shop, all coffee are brewed. No sugar added. No creamer. And the last and best of all, no customers. You can be alone however long you want.
